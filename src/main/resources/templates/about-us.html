About Us

FurBabyFinder.Site is an open source project that provides a free and low-cost technology application to the animal welfare community. FurBabyFinder.Site is an unaffiliated, volunteer-driven, non-competitive group that wants to work with like-minded organizations to help animal welfare organizations, volunteers and pets.
